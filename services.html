Our Services

End-to-end support for hardware brands.

From identifying the right factory to refining a product specification, we work alongside your team across every step of the sourcing and development journey.

01 / Sourcing

Vendor Sourcing

Finding the right manufacturer is the difference between a product that scales and one that stalls. We've spent over a decade building relationships with factories across China, Taiwan, Vietnam, and the broader East Asian region — and we put that network to work for you.

  • Supplier identification and shortlisting based on your spec, volume, and quality bar
  • Factory audits, capability assessments, and on-site visits
  • Sample evaluation, pricing negotiation, and MOQ alignment
  • Production oversight and quality control coordination
02 / Design

Product Design & Development

Great hardware is invisible when it works and unforgettable when it doesn't. We help brands design products that perform — combining engineering rigor with an eye for the details that matter to architects, designers, and end users.

  • Concept development and design ideation for new hardware lines
  • Production-ready CAD, 3D modeling, and technical drawings
  • Material specification, finish selection, and prototyping
  • Design-for-manufacture refinement with the chosen factory
03 / Partnership

Partner Matching

The right partner doesn't just ship product — they grow with you. Our matching process goes beyond price comparison to look at culture fit, communication style, technical capability, and long-term capacity, so you build relationships that last.

  • Long-term factory partnership selection aligned to your scale plan
  • Contract review, IP protection, and tooling ownership clarity
  • Multi-supplier strategy for risk diversification
  • Ongoing relationship management and dispute resolution
